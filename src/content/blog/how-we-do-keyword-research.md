Good blog posts start from real demand. Here is the lightweight process we use.

## 1. Start from a seed

Pick a topic your audience cares about. Expand it with autocomplete and
"people also ask" style questions.

## 2. Pull real volumes

We use **DataForSEO** to get search volume, difficulty, and related keywords.
From an AI editor with the DataForSEO MCP connected, you can ask for keyword
ideas and metrics directly — no spreadsheets.

## 3. Map one post per intent

Each keyword cluster becomes one post with a clear intent (informational,
comparison, or transactional). Write the post, publish, and let Search Console
confirm which queries actually land.

## 4. Close the loop

After a few weeks, Search Console shows the real queries bringing traffic.
Write follow-up posts targeting the rising ones.
